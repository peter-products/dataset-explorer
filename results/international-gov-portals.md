## Coverage Notes

### G20 Countries (non-US)
All covered: Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China (limited open data), France, Germany, India, Indonesia, Italy, Japan, Mexico, Russia, Saudi Arabia, South Korea, Turkey, UK, EU.

Note: China does not have a single centralized national open data portal comparable to other G20 nations. Provincial/city-level portals exist (e.g., Shanghai, Beijing) but a unified national portal is not publicly accessible in the same way.

### EU Member States (27)
All 27 EU member states have national open data portals, all federated into the European Data Portal (data.europa.eu). Coverage above includes: Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Ireland, Italy, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Netherlands, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden.

### Key Resources for Discovery
- **DataPortals.org** (https://dataportals.org) - most comprehensive global directory of open data portals
- **Open Data Barometer** (https://opendatabarometer.org) - ranks countries on open data readiness and implementation
- **ODIN** (https://odin.opendatawatch.com) - Open Data Inventory covering 197 countries
- **European Data Portal Maturity Report** (https://data.europa.eu/en/open-data-maturity/2025) - annual EU open data maturity assessment
