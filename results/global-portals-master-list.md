## PLATFORM-BASED DISCOVERY NOTES

### Socrata (Tyler Technologies)
- Powers hundreds of government open data portals
- Discovery API at `api.us.socrata.com/api/catalog/v1/domains` returns all domains
- Major users: NYC, Chicago, Seattle, most US states, many US cities
- **TODO**: Fetch full domain list from API (was returning 404 on initial attempt — try alternate endpoints)

### CKAN
- Open source, used by many national portals (Australia, Japan, Italy, Argentina, Canada)
- Instance registry at ckan.org/instances
- Check `/api/3/action/package_list` on any suspected CKAN site

### ArcGIS Hub (Esri)
- Major platform for GIS/geospatial open data
- hub.arcgis.com hosts thousands of organization sites
- Used by: Washington DC, Portland, Phoenix, Boise, Cape Town, Arizona state, many counties

### OpenDataSoft
- Now Huwise — hosts 2,900+ portal listing
- data.opendatasoft.com (now hub.huwise.com)
- Used by many European cities and some US cities

### DKAN (Drupal-based)
- Used by Jamaica, Mauritius, some others
- Fewer instances than CKAN/Socrata
